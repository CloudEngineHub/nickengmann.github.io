PiSight - 2016

PiSight is a platform that can analyze the mac addresses of all nearby devices that are wifi enabled. By identifying the nearby devices you can both keep track of inflow and outflow of individuals in networked locations. This is a cheap way for cities or governments to analyze secure locations in cases of emergencies. This technology also has the ability to triangulate locations of wifi enabled devices.

# Technical Specifics:
Raspberry Pi. Scripts Programmed in Python. Server ran on a Flask backend with Python scripts to do calculations

Sentiment - 2015

Sentiment is a Plantronics sponsored application which identifies human emotion in speech. The application offers its user an emotion recognition analysis service by extracting vocal parameters indicative of emotional states for incoming audio through a phone call. Sentiment may provide audio and display notifications to the user based on the user's desired Settings.This project spanned from Fall 2014 - Spring 2015 as part of my senior design project. I developed Sentiment in conjunction with a team of five students and a faculty mentor. My main contribution to this project was dealing with the initial speech processing and developing the algorithm using a Java based machine learning platform.

Mr. Lockman - 2015

Mr. Lockman deals with the modern day problem of security. If you lose your keys then you are unfortunately locked out of your house/home. Major League Qudditch

I have been involved with Major League Quidditch (MLQ), the official league for collegiate and community quidditch since 2012. I have helped officiate gameplay as a snitch. For anyone who is unfamiliar with Quidditch- as I snitch I am a pivotal element in every Quidditch game. I am realesed close to the end of the game and my job is to try and run around and not allow anyone from either team to capture me as for as long as I possibly can. If I am caught the team who caught me recieves 30 points and the end of the game is then signaled. Because snitches end the course of the game and are worth 30 points they are heavily contested. As a snitch my job is to not only ensure entertaining and prolonged gameplay but it is also to look out for the health and safety of the players who are attempting to capture me. I take my role as a Quidditch snitch very seriously and because of that I have achieved the Gold Level" certification status. This status allows me to snitch major tournaments including regional and national games.
